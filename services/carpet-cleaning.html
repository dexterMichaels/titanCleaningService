Our Process

How We Clean Your Carpets

Every carpet cleaning job follows our proven 5-step process to deliver consistent, thorough results.

Inspection & Pre-Treatment

We begin by inspecting your carpets to identify fibre type, soiling levels, and any problem areas. High-traffic zones and stains receive a targeted pre-treatment solution to break down embedded dirt before cleaning begins.

Deep Carpet Cleaning

Using our professional low-moisture system, we deep clean the entire carpet. Counter-rotating brushes agitate the cleaning solution into the fibres, lifting dirt, allergens, and bacteria from the base of the carpet.

Stain & Odor Removal

Stubborn spots, pet stains, and odors receive additional treatment with specialized solutions. Enzyme-based treatments break down organic matter at the molecular level for complete removal.

Fast Drying

Because our system uses minimal moisture, your carpets begin drying immediately. Most carpets are fully dry and ready for normal use within 1–2 hours of cleaning.

Final Grooming

We finish by grooming the carpet pile with a professional rake to ensure an even, uniform appearance. We do a final walk-through with you to make sure you’re completely satisfied.
