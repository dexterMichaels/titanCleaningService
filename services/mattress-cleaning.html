Our Process

How We Clean Your Mattress

Every mattress cleaning follows our proven 5-step process for thorough, safe results on any mattress type.

Inspection

We examine your mattress to identify the type (memory foam, innerspring, hybrid, latex), note any stains, odors, or areas of concern, and determine the best cleaning approach for your specific mattress.

Dry Vacuuming

Using a professional-grade HEPA vacuum, we thoroughly vacuum the entire mattress surface, sides, and seams to remove loose dust, dead skin cells, dust mites, and surface debris before wet cleaning begins.

Spot & Stain Treatment

Visible stains from sweat, blood, urine, and spills are pre-treated with targeted enzyme-based solutions. These products break down organic matter at the molecular level for maximum stain removal without harsh chemicals.

Deep Extraction Cleaning

Our low-moisture extraction system deep cleans the mattress fabric and upper layers, pulling out embedded dirt, allergens, and cleaning solution. This method cleans effectively while using minimal water to protect the mattress interior.

Deodorizing & Drying

We apply an eco-friendly deodorizer to neutralize any remaining odors, then ensure the mattress has proper airflow for quick drying. Most mattresses are ready for use within 2 to 4 hours of cleaning.

Mattress Cleaning Questions

Common questions about our professional mattress cleaning service.

We recommend professional mattress cleaning every 6 to 12 months for most households. If you suffer from allergies, asthma, or have pets that sleep on the bed, more frequent cleaning every 3 to 6 months can help reduce allergen buildup and improve sleep quality.
A single mattress typically takes 30 to 45 minutes to clean, depending on size and condition. If there are heavy stains that require extra spot treatment, it may take up to an hour. We work efficiently to get your bed back in service as quickly as possible.
