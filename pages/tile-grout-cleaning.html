Our Process

How We Clean Your Tile & Grout

Every tile and grout cleaning follows our thorough 5-step process for consistent, professional results.

Assessment & Pre-Treatment

We inspect your tile and grout to identify the tile type, grout condition, and any problem areas. A professional-grade alkaline or pH-neutral pre-treatment solution is applied to break down embedded dirt, grease, and buildup in the grout lines.

High-Pressure Steam Extraction

Using our truck-mounted or portable high-pressure steam system, we blast hot water into the grout lines and tile surface at controlled pressure. This loosens and extracts deep-seated dirt, mold, and bacteria that regular mopping cannot reach.

Grout Line Detailing

After the main extraction, we go over each grout line with specialized brushes and tools to ensure every inch is thoroughly cleaned. Stubborn stains and discoloration receive targeted treatment for maximum restoration.

Rinse & Dry

We rinse the entire surface with clean water to remove any remaining cleaning solution and residue. Excess moisture is extracted, and the floor is left clean and ready to walk on within 1 to 2 hours.

Optional Sealer Application

For long-lasting protection, we offer a professional grout sealer application. The sealer penetrates into the grout to create a barrier against future staining, moisture absorption, and mold growth — extending the life of your clean.
