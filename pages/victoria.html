Carpet Cleaning & More in Victoria

Titan Cleaning Service is Victoria’s trusted choice for professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, and a full range of residential and commercial cleaning services.

As a locally owned and operated cleaning company based in Victoria, BC, we understand the unique needs of homes and businesses in this community. The Pacific Northwest climate means carpets, upholstery, and exterior surfaces face moisture, mud, and organic buildup year-round. Our professional cleaning services are designed to address exactly these challenges.

We use advanced low-moisture carpet cleaning technology that delivers a deeper clean with faster drying times — typically 1 to 2 hours instead of the 12+ hours required by traditional steam cleaning. All of our cleaning products are eco-friendly and safe for children, pets, and sensitive environments. Whether you need carpet cleaning in Victoria for your family home or a commercial space, Titan delivers professional results backed by our satisfaction guarantee.
