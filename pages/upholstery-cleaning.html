Our Process

How We Clean Your Upholstery

Every upholstery cleaning follows our proven 5-step process for consistent, thorough results.

Fabric Assessment

We inspect each piece to identify the fabric type, check manufacturer care codes, and note any areas of concern such as heavy staining, wear, or delicate materials that require special attention.

Pre-Treatment

We apply a fabric-safe pre-treatment solution to break down embedded dirt, oils, and stains. High-traffic areas and visible spots receive extra attention with targeted stain-lifting agents.

Deep Extraction Cleaning

Using our professional-grade low-moisture extraction system, we deep clean the upholstery fibers to remove dirt, allergens, and cleaning solution. This method cleans thoroughly while minimizing dry time.

Stain Treatment

Remaining stubborn stains are treated individually with specialized spotting solutions. We address food, beverage, ink, pet, and other common upholstery stains without damaging the fabric.

Drying & Inspection

We do a final walk-through with you to ensure every piece meets your expectations. We position furniture for optimal airflow and provide care tips to keep your upholstery looking fresh longer.
