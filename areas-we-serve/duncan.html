Cleaning Services for the Cowichan Valley

Titan Cleaning Service proudly extends our professional cleaning services to Duncan and the wider Cowichan Valley — from rural hobby farms to growing commercial spaces in the heart of downtown.

The Cowichan Valley is one of Vancouver Island’s most diverse regions, blending small-town character with a thriving agricultural community. From the horse farms and wineries that line the valley floor to the heritage homes and newer subdivisions in and around Duncan, properties here face cleaning challenges that are distinct from urban areas. Larger floor plans, mudrooms that see constant use, and carpets that bear the wear of rural living all demand professional attention that goes beyond standard household cleaning.

The Cowichan Valley’s warm, humid climate — the warmest in all of Canada — creates ideal conditions for moisture retention in carpets and upholstery. Mold, mildew, and allergens accumulate faster in valley homes, especially in older heritage properties where ventilation may be limited. Our low-moisture cleaning technology is particularly well-suited to this environment: carpets dry in 1 to 2 hours rather than 12+, reducing the window for mold growth and getting your home back to normal quickly. Every product we use is eco-friendly and safe for families, pets, and the environment — something that matters deeply to the Cowichan Valley’s conservation-minded community.
