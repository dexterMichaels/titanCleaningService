Cleaning Services for Mill Bay & South Cowichan

Titan Cleaning Service provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, tile and grout restoration, and pressure washing for homeowners and businesses throughout Mill Bay, Cobble Hill, Shawnigan Lake, and the South Cowichan area.

Mill Bay is the gateway to the Cowichan Valley, a scenic community perched along the shores of Saanich Inlet where rural tranquility meets easy access to both Victoria and Duncan. Connected to the Saanich Peninsula by the BC Ferries route from Brentwood Bay, Mill Bay attracts residents who value space, privacy, and the slower pace of South Cowichan living. From waterfront properties along the inlet to the hobby farms and acreages that dot the hillsides above the Trans-Canada Highway, homes here tend to be larger and see unique cleaning challenges — mudrooms that handle constant traffic, carpets that absorb moisture from the surrounding forest, and exterior surfaces that collect moss and organic debris year-round.

The South Cowichan area — including the charming communities of Cobble Hill and Shawnigan Lake — shares Mill Bay’s rural character and its cleaning demands. Shawnigan Lake’s lakeside cottages and vacation properties need regular deep cleaning, while Cobble Hill’s growing population of young families and retirees keeps demand high for carpet and upholstery care. Titan’s low-moisture cleaning system is perfectly suited to this environment: carpets dry in 1–2 hours, reducing the risk of mold in these moisture-rich settings, and our eco-friendly products are safe for the families, pets, and rural properties that define South Cowichan living. Backed by our satisfaction guarantee and a perfect 5-star Google rating from over 100 customers across Vancouver Island, Titan is your trusted cleaning service in Mill Bay and the surrounding area.
