Cleaning Services for Langford & the West Shore

Titan Cleaning Service provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, tile and grout restoration, and pressure washing for homeowners and businesses throughout Langford, Colwood, and the West Shore communities.

Langford is one of the fastest-growing cities in British Columbia, and its mix of established neighbourhoods and brand-new developments creates a wide range of cleaning needs. From the family homes around Langford Lake and Glen Lake to the newer subdivisions of Westhills and Happy Valley, homeowners here are investing in their properties and expect professional-grade results. Whether you’re maintaining a busy household with kids and pets, preparing a new home for move-in, or keeping a rental property guest-ready, Titan’s eco-friendly, fast-dry cleaning methods are designed for the demands of West Shore living.

The West Shore is also home to a thriving commercial sector — from the retail centres along Goldstream Avenue and Veterans Memorial Parkway to the restaurants and businesses at Bear Mountain. Titan serves commercial properties of all sizes with tile and grout restoration, floor waxing and stripping, carpet cleaning, and pressure washing. The region’s proximity to Goldstream Provincial Park means moss, algae, and moisture buildup are common on driveways, patios, and exterior surfaces, making regular pressure washing essential. Backed by our satisfaction guarantee and a perfect 5-star Google rating from over 100 customers across Vancouver Island, Titan delivers reliable, professional results throughout Langford and the entire West Shore.
