Oak Bay BC

Cleaning Services for Oak Bay

Titan Cleaning Service provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, tile and grout restoration, and pressure washing for homeowners and businesses throughout the District of Oak Bay.

Oak Bay is one of Greater Victoria’s most distinguished communities, known for its charming Tudor-style homes, manicured gardens, and quiet, tree-canopied streets. Many of Oak Bay’s residences date back to the early 1900s, featuring hardwood floors, ornate tile work, and high-quality wool and Persian carpets that demand specialized care. Whether you’re maintaining a heritage home along Beach Drive, a character house near the Uplands, or a well-appointed condo on Oak Bay Avenue, Titan delivers the kind of careful, detail-oriented cleaning that Oak Bay residents expect — without harsh chemicals or excessive moisture that could damage fine furnishings and vintage materials.

Oak Bay’s proximity to the ocean and its mature tree canopy create a unique set of cleaning challenges. Salt air accelerates wear on upholstery and carpets, while moss, lichen, and leaf debris accumulate on driveways, stone patios, and cedar decks throughout the year. The village centre along Oak Bay Avenue — with its independent shops, tea rooms, and restaurants — relies on clean, presentable floors and storefronts to maintain the area’s renowned character. From the waterfront estates of the Uplands to the family homes around Willows Beach and Estevan Village, Titan provides eco-friendly, professional cleaning backed by our 5-star Google rating and satisfaction guarantee.
