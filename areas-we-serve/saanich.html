Saanich BC

Cleaning Services for the District of Saanich

Titan Cleaning Service provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, tile and grout restoration, and pressure washing for homeowners and businesses throughout the District of Saanich — Greater Victoria’s largest municipality.

Saanich is the most populous municipality in the Capital Regional District, home to over 120,000 residents across a wide range of neighbourhoods — from the established, tree-lined streets of Gordon Head and Cordova Bay to the bustling commercial centres of Tillicum and Quadra. With its mix of mid-century ranchers, newer townhome developments, and sprawling rural properties in the highlands, Saanich presents a diverse range of cleaning needs. Whether you’re dealing with high-traffic carpets in a busy family home in Royal Oak, pet hair and allergens in a Broadmead living room, or moss-covered patios in the wooded areas of Prospect Lake, Titan has the equipment and expertise to deliver professional results.

Saanich’s green, park-rich landscape — including Mount Douglas, Elk and Beaver Lake, and the Galloping Goose Trail — makes it one of the most desirable places to live on Vancouver Island. But the lush surroundings also mean elevated moisture, moss growth on exterior surfaces, and organic debris tracked into homes year-round. Our eco-friendly, low-moisture cleaning methods are ideal for this environment: carpets dry in 1–2 hours, reducing the risk of mold and mildew, and our pressure washing service removes years of moss and algae buildup from driveways, walkways, and decks. Saanich is our home turf — we’re here regularly and offer fast scheduling with same-day appointments often available.
