Cleaning Services for Sidney & the Saanich Peninsula

Titan Cleaning Service provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, tile and grout restoration, and pressure washing for homeowners and businesses throughout Sidney, North Saanich, and the wider Saanich Peninsula.

Sidney is a charming seaside town at the northern tip of the Saanich Peninsula, known for its relaxed pace, stunning waterfront, and thriving retirement community. Many residents here take great pride in maintaining clean, comfortable homes — and that’s where Titan comes in. Whether you live in a waterfront condo overlooking the marina, a single-family home near Beacon Avenue, or a larger estate property in North Saanich, our professional cleaning services are tailored to the specific needs of Peninsula living. The proximity to the ocean means salt air, elevated humidity, and moisture-driven buildup on carpets, upholstery, and exterior surfaces that require specialized attention.

Sidney’s role as the gateway to the Gulf Islands — with the BC Ferries terminal at Swartz Bay and the Washington State Ferry at the town wharf — also makes it a hub for vacation rentals, bed and breakfasts, and hospitality businesses that depend on quick-turnaround cleaning between guests. The town’s celebrated “Booktown” downtown, lined with independent bookshops and boutiques, creates demand for reliable commercial cleaning as well. From the quiet acreages of Deep Cove and Lands End to the bustling communities of Brentwood Bay and Saanichton, Titan delivers eco-friendly, fast-dry cleaning results backed by our satisfaction guarantee and a perfect 5-star Google rating from over 100 customers across Vancouver Island.
